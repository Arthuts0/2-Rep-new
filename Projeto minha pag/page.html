The social service!

The church's social service in Africa is a testimony of faith in action, reflecting a commitment to the well-being and dignity of the most vulnerable communities. Through initiatives such as food distribution, healthcare, education and psychological support, the church seeks to meet urgent needs, promoting hope and transformation. Each project is a step towards a more just and inclusive future, where the love and compassion of Christ are manifested in practice, bringing relief and renewal to the lives that need it most.
